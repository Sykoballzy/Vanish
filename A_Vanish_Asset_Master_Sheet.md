## NAMING GOTCHAS (fix these before uploading)
- The file that came in named `hero-hunter-bow.PNG` is actually the **crouched-by-tree** shot → rename to `field-concealed-03.png`.
- Your real hero (walking hunter with bow) came in as `1000010216.PNG` → rename to `hero-hunter-bow.png`.
- `art_moose-v.PNG` uses an underscore → change to `art-moose-v.png` (hyphen) to match the pattern.
- Two different deer graphics both wanted "deer-v" — the whitetail-sunset is `art-deer-v.png`; the mule-deer-studio is `art-muledeer-v.png`. Don't let them overwrite each other.
