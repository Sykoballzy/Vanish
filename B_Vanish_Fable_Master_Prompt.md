> Build a complete, premium e-commerce website for a hunting apparel brand: **VANISH APPAREL**. Tagline: **"BUILT FOR THE HUNT."**
>
> ## Assets provided — use them, do not generate stock imagery. Map as follows:
> - **Primary logo** (header, footer, favicon): the white angular chevron-V + "VANISH" wordmark, horizontal version. Do not bake it onto a background — place the clean white logo over the brand's own photography.
> - **Full lockup** (stacked "VANISH / APPAREL / BUILT FOR THE HUNT" with the broadhead icon): About page, loading screen, footer email block. Use the broadhead icon as the favicon and as a subtle section divider.
> - **Hero image:** the photo of the hunter walking through brush with a bow (`hero-hunter-bow`).
> - **Concealment gallery:** the three field photos of a concealed hunter (`field-concealed-01/02/03`).
> - **"Find the hunter" proof section:** the wide terrain shot where the hunter is barely visible (`field-concealed-04-wide`), full-bleed with overlaid text.
> - **DEADFALL camo feature:** the dark studio flat-lay (`deadfall-kit-studio`) on the homepage; the full laid-out loadout with swatches (`deadfall-loadout-full`) on the Shop page header.
> - **Gear collection feature:** the branded jacket/pack/beanie on the mannequin (`collection-mannequin-branded`).
> - **Story / section-break art:** the skull poster (`art-hero-skull-poster`) as the About hero and email-band background; the wildlife-in-V illustrations (deer/elk/moose) as section breaks.
>
> ## Brand feel
> Premium-technical base, warmed with rugged wildlife art and real field photography. Aesthetic tier of Sitka and First Lite, but with more American warmth. Confident, earned, not flashy.
>
> ## Visual system
> - Base: charcoal / near-black (#141414). Text: bone white (#F2EFE9).
> - Single accent: warm amber-orange (#C8702D) for buttons, links, and key highlights. Use sparingly.
> - Headlines: bold condensed technical sans-serif matching the angular VANISH wordmark. Body: clean, readable sans-serif.
> - Full-bleed imagery, strong grid, generous spacing, subtle scroll-reveal animation. Mobile-first — most hunters browse on phones.
>
> ## Signature detail
> The brand's camo pattern is named **DEADFALL**. Treat it as a proprietary named pattern throughout (like Sitka's Optifade). Reference it on product pages and give it a dedicated homepage feature section explaining the concealment design.
>
> ## Pages
>
> **1. Home**
> - Full-bleed hero (`hero-hunter-bow`) with "BUILT FOR THE HUNT" and a "Shop the Collection" CTA.
> - Feature strip: Silent Fabric / Scent Control / All-Weather Built.
> - DEADFALL camo feature section (`deadfall-kit-studio` + short copy).
> - Featured products grid (6 items — mix of merch and a "Coming Soon" gear teaser).
> - Full-bleed "Find the hunter" proof section (`field-concealed-04-wide`) with overlaid text: "Find the hunter. Good luck."
> - Brand-story teaser → links to About.
> - **Field Wins** section: a curated gallery grid of hunter success photos with the CTA "Tag #wevanish to get featured."
> - Email signup band (background: `art-hero-skull-poster`).
>
> **2. Shop — TWO TIERS, both live at launch**
> - **Merch (buy now, full checkout):** T-Shirts, Hats, Hoodies, Cups. Each has size (S–XXL where applicable) and color/style variant selectors and an "Add to Cart" button.
> - **Gear (Coming Soon):** the DEADFALL apparel line, shown as a "Coming Soon" collection. Each gear item displays a **"Notify Me"** email-capture button instead of "Add to Cart," feeding a launch list.
> - Category filters across both tiers.
>
> **3. Product Detail** — image gallery, name, price, description, variant selectors (size + color), "Add to Cart" (or "Notify Me" for gear), and a specs accordion (Materials, Care, Sizing).
>
> **4. Field Wins** (own page) — the curated success-photo wall as a gallery grid, a "Tag us #wevanish to get featured" callout, and a "Submit Your Hunt" button linking to Instagram. Build as a manually-populated gallery, NOT a live social feed.
>
> **5. About** — use the provided brand story copy (below). Pair with `art-hero-skull-poster` as the page hero and wildlife-in-V art as section breaks.
>
> **6. Contact** — form (Name, Email, Subject, Message), support email, social links.
>
> **7. Cart / Checkout** — standard cart summary and checkout flow.
>
> ## Global
> - **Header:** logo left; nav center (Home / Shop / Field Wins / About / Contact); cart icon right.
> - **Footer:** full lockup, tagline, nav links, social icons (Instagram / Facebook / YouTube / TikTok), email signup, copyright.
>
> ## Copy tone
> Short, punchy, confident. No corporate fluff. Headline energy: "Disappear. Then Dominate." / "Engineered for the Silent Stalk." / "Stay Cool. Stay Hidden." / "Wear the Woods."
>

> **BUILT FROM THE GROUND UP. AND THE MOUNTAINS DOWN.**

## AFTER FABLE GENERATES — how to iterate
Fable does best with one strong prompt plus targeted follow-ups. Don't try to fix everything in the first prompt. Generate, then send single-focus fixes like:
- "Make the hero text larger and left-aligned."
- "The DEADFALL feature section background is too busy — darken the overlay."
- "Move the Field Wins section above the featured products."

One change at a time gets cleaner results than a rewrite.
